🎡 About This Project

A real-time decision tool to help you earn passive income on BTC using the Wheel options strategy

💡 Why We Built This

You might be familiar with spot trading, futures, or even DeFi farming, but "options" sounds like another language — Greek letters, implied volatility, strike prices...

In fact, there's an options strategy called Wheel that's incredibly simple:

When you have USDT, sell Puts (get paid to wait for a dip). When you hold BTC, sell Calls (get paid to wait for a pump). No matter what the market does, you collect "premium" at every step — free extra income.

The question is: when to sell? Which contract? Is volatility high enough? Is the market fearful or greedy?

Checking Deribit's DVOL, Binance prices, Alternative.me's Fear & Greed Index, then manually calculating annualized yield and assignment probability — it's exhausting.

This tool automates all of that. Open the page, see what to do at a glance.

🧭 Core Philosophy

Sell Puts when others are fearful (buy the dip), sell Calls when others are greedy (sell the top), collect premium at every step.

This isn't about predicting price direction — it's about earning extra premium on prices you'd buy/sell at anyway. The Wheel strategy turns "waiting" into "income."
